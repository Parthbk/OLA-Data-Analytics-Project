# OLA Ride Insights Analytics Project

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing over 100,000+ OLA ride records to uncover customer behavior, ride demand trends, operational inefficiencies, and revenue-driving factors. Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, the project delivers actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and data analysis.

The objective of this project is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying peak demand periods, cancellation trends, payment preferences, vehicle usage patterns, and service quality metrics.

## Key Business Insights

- Identified peak ride demand hours during:
  - Morning: 8 AM – 10 AM
  - Evening: 6 PM – 9 PM

- Analyzed customer behavior and ride booking patterns across different vehicle categories

- Observed higher driver-side cancellations compared to customer cancellations, indicating operational inefficiencies

- Found Cash as the most preferred payment method among users

- Identified Prime Sedan as the most frequently booked vehicle type

- Analyzed customer and driver ratings (~4.0 average) to evaluate service quality and customer satisfaction

- Performed data cleaning and preprocessing to improve accuracy and reliability of analysis
